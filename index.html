ABOUT

Orange City School District is a top rated, private school district located in CLEVELAND, OH. It has 2,042 students in grades PK, K-12 with a student-teacher ratio of 16 to 1. According to state test scores, 97% of students are at least proficient in math and 99% in reading. The Orange City School District is located on the east side of Greater Cleveland on a 1176-acre campus at 32000 Chagrin Blvd. in Pepper Pike, Ohio. The District serves the five municipalities of Hunting Valley, Moreland Hills, Orange Village, Pepper Pike and Woodmere along with small portions of Bedford Heights, Solon, and Warrensville Heights. School bus in front of OHS The District empowers our students, staff and community to be passionate and innovative global citizens committed to excellence in learning, leading and making a difference. We place a high degree of importance on ensuring that all children have the opportunity to reach their full potential inside and outside of the classroom. This profile helps characterize the overall educational value of the Orange City School District in areas that matter most to our community. We offer a rigourous curriculum to ensure students achieve well on both assessements and innovative learning experiences.
